# Business Requirements

## 1. Business Objectives

Acme Manufacturing Ltd. requires a secure enterprise document management and collaboration platform to improve how employees store, share, and access business documents across multiple locations.

The organisation intends to use Nextcloud as a central platform to enable secure collaboration while maintaining control over sensitive business information.

The primary objectives are:

- Provide employees with secure access to corporate documents from office and remote locations.
- Improve collaboration between internal teams and approved external partners.
- Reduce reliance on unmanaged file-sharing solutions.
- Maintain confidentiality, integrity, and availability of business information.
- Establish a scalable platform that can support future organisational growth.

---

## 2. Business Drivers

The key drivers for deploying Nextcloud are:

- Increasing requirement for secure remote working capabilities.
- Need for centralised document storage and collaboration.
- Reduction of data leakage risks from unmanaged file-sharing platforms.
- Improved visibility and control over document access.
- Ability to enforce organisational security policies.

---

## 3. Stakeholders

The key stakeholders involved in the deployment include:

| Stakeholder | Responsibility |
|------------|----------------|
| Executive Management | Business ownership and strategic decisions |
| IT Operations Team | Platform deployment and maintenance |
| Security Team | Security architecture, risk management, and monitoring |
| Employees | Day-to-day document access and collaboration |
| Department Managers | Access approval and data ownership |
| External Suppliers | Controlled access to shared documents |

---

## 4. User Types

The platform will support the following user groups:

| User Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Standard Employees | Access and collaborate on business documents |
| Department Managers | Manage team documents and approve access |
| IT Administrators | Maintain and configure the platform |
| Security Administrators | Monitor security events and investigate incidents |
| External Partners | Limited access to specifically shared documents |

---

## 5. Business Assets

The following business assets will be stored or managed within the platform:

- Engineering documentation and product designs.
- Internal business documents.
- Contracts and supplier information.
- Human resources documents.
- Financial and operational reports.
- Project collaboration files.

---

## 6. Functional Requirements

The platform must support:

- Secure document storage and retrieval.
- File sharing between authorised users.
- Collaboration between teams.
- Remote access capabilities.
- User and group management.
- Document versioning and recovery.
- Integration with enterprise identity services.

---

## 7. Non-Functional Requirements

The solution should provide:

- Scalability to support approximately 5,000 users.
- High availability for business-critical documents.
- Reliable backup and recovery capabilities.
- Acceptable performance for users across multiple locations.
- Maintainability by internal IT teams.

---

## 8. Security Requirements

The platform must:

- Enforce strong authentication mechanisms.
- Apply role-based access control.
- Protect sensitive data during storage and transmission.
- Maintain audit logs for important user activities.
- Support security monitoring and incident investigation.
- Follow the principle of least privilege.

---

## 9. Compliance Requirements

The deployment should consider:

- Data protection requirements.
- Internal security policies.
- Industry security best practices.
- Applicable regulatory obligations based on business operations.

---

## 10. Assumptions and Constraints

Assumptions:

- Users will access the platform using corporate-managed devices.
- Existing enterprise identity services are available.
- Security monitoring capabilities exist within the organisation.

Constraints:

- The solution should minimise operational complexity.
- The organisation requires a cost-effective solution using open-source technology.
- The deployment must support future expansion.
