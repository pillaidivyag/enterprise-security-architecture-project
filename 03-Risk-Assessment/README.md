# Risk Assessment

## Overview

Following the threat modelling exercise, each identified threat was assessed to determine its potential impact on the business and the level of risk it presents.

The assessment helps prioritise remediation activities before production deployment.

---

## Documents

- Risk Assessment Methodology
- Risk Register
- Risk Matrix
- Business Impact Assessment
- Risk Treatment Plan

---

## Assessment Outcome

12 threats assessed

High Risks: 6

Medium Risks: 4

Low Risks: 2

The highest risks relate to identity compromise, unauthorised access to confidential documents and application security.
